Google Scholar

I'm a second-year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore, working on AI/machine learning. I'm interested in sequential decision-making under uncertainty (e.g., Bayesian optimization, reinforcement learning, multi-armed bandit and active learning), and automated machine learning. Previously, I also worked on computational neuroscience.
